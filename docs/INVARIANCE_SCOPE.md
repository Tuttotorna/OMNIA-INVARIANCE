# OMNIA-INVARIANCE Scope

## Purpose

OMNIA-INVARIANCE studies structural behavior under transformation.

The central thesis is:

```text
Structural truth = invariance under transformation
```

This does not mean that structural invariance automatically proves semantic truth.

It means that stability under controlled transformation is a measurable structural signal.

## Boundary

```text
measurement != inference != decision
structural invariance != semantic truth
```

## What is in scope

OMNIA-INVARIANCE studies:

- perturbation trajectories
- raw trajectory geometry
- normalized trajectory geometry
- topology robustness
- cross-domain structural similarity
- trajectory classification
- synthetic stress testing
- representation-dependent behavior
- invariance and collapse under transformation

## What is out of scope

OMNIA-INVARIANCE does not certify:

- semantic equivalence
- universal physical identity
- final truth
- production safety
- model intelligence
- domain correctness without external review

## Correct interpretation

```text
stable under transformation = structural evidence
unstable under transformation = structural fragility
neither result is a final semantic decision
```
