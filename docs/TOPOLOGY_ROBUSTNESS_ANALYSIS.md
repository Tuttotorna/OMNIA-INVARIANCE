# OMNIA-INVARIANCE — Topology Robustness Analysis

## Core Idea

Trajectory-space should not be evaluated
under only a single geometric representation.

Different transformations may reveal:

- stable structures
- unstable clusters
- scale artifacts
- persistent neighbors
- topology drift

The framework therefore introduces:

Topology Robustness Analysis

---

# Core Question

Does trajectory-space remain structurally coherent
across multiple geometric constructions?

Or does topology collapse
under representation changes?

---

- emphasizes structural shape

# Embedding Drift

A system may change geometric position
across spaces.

Large drift may indicate:

- scale dependence
- unstable topology
- representation sensitivity

Small drift may indicate:

- persistent structural geometry
