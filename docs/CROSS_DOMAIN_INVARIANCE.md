# CROSS-DOMAIN INVARIANCE

## Core question

```text
Can different systems exhibit similar structural stability patterns
across representation layers?

OMNIA-INVARIANCE does not compare meaning.

It compares:

structural persistence

coherence collapse

irreversibility

representation-dependent stability


across unrelated domains.


---

Core principle

Different systems can appear unstable or stable depending on the representation layer used to observe them.

This repository studies whether this phenomenon itself is structurally recurrent across domains.


---

Minimal abstraction

system
↓
representation layers
↓
controlled perturbation
↓
structural measurement
↓
stability profile

The goal is not prediction.

The goal is structural characterization.

Central observation

surface similarity
does not guarantee
structural persistence

dynamical systems

---

Important boundary

This repository does not claim:

universal ontology

proof of consciousness

universal instability law

equivalence between domains

replacement for physics or logic


The framework only measures structural behavior under perturbation.
