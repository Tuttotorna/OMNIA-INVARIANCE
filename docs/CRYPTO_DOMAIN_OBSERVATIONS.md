- high initial coherence

# Structural Interpretation

The crypto-domain trajectory differs from the physics profile and the logic profile in one important aspect:

```text
collapse acceleration
```

The structure remains highly stable during early perturbations, but degradation accelerates rapidly once structural thresholds are crossed.

This behavior resembles systems designed around strict dependency constraints.

---

# Possible Structural Reading

Current observations suggest:

## Phase 1 — Local Resistance

Small perturbations produce limited structural degradation.

The system absorbs noise while preserving coherence.

---

## Phase 2 — Threshold Region

Structural stress accumulates.

Recovery capacity weakens.

Instability begins propagating across dependencies.

---

## Phase 3 — Rapid Collapse

Once critical dependencies fail, coherence drops quickly and irreversibility rises sharply.

The degradation becomes difficult to recover.
